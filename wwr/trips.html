Know our best adventures!

The Ripple Run

(Beginners Friendly)

A gentle and scenic ideal for first-timers and families with kids. Cruise along mild currents, admire the natural landscape, and enjoy splashy fun without the high-intensity of the River Rapids.

Class: I-II | Duration: 1 hour | Great for: Families, kids, casual explorers

Canyon Drift

(Intermediate Challenge)

Wind through narrow canyons and foaming rapids on this mi-level adventure. With thrilling twists and turns, this route balances fun and excitement without overwhelming newcomers.

Class: II-III | Duration: 2 hours | Great for: Couples, friend groups, team building.

Thunder Gorge

(Advanced adrenaline)

Our most intense and heart-pounding adventure. For true thrill-seekers, this wild ride features roaring rapids, big drops, and rapid-fire twists through a rugged river canyon.

Class: III-IV | Duration: 3 hours | Great for: Experts, professional riders, and adrenaline junkies.
